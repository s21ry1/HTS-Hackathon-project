About Our Map

In this map, we are highlighting the Bhilai area affected by mosquito-borne diseases, particularly dengue. The red markers indicate areas where citizens have been most affected, categorized by their respective pin codes.

The purpose of this map is to provide a visual representation of the Bhilai area's susceptibility to mosquito-borne illnesses, with a specific focus on dengue outbreaks. The prominently featured red-marked regions denote areas where a significant portion of the local population has been impacted by these diseases, and they have been further categorized based on their individual pin codes for better clarity and analysis.
